Halfway through the year, it's time to pause and reflect. Setting goals is the easy part — the hard part is maintaining momentum when life inevitably gets in the way.

## What's Working

- **Consistent morning routine** — Waking up early and journaling has been transformative
- **Reading habit** — 18 books so far this year
- **Side projects** — Shipped two small tools that people actually use

## What Needs Work

- **Exercise consistency** — Started strong, fell off in March
- **Writing more** — This blog is part of the solution
- **Saying no** — Still taking on too many commitments

## The Adjusted Plan

The key insight is that goals need to be living documents. What made sense in January doesn't always make sense in July. Adaptability isn't failure — it's wisdom.
