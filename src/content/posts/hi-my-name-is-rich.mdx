Hi, my name is Rich and I'm not ok. The past 3 years have been rough: I got divorced... Left my wife for what I thought was love. I lost my partner. Lost my home. Lost good friends. Two best friends as a matter of fact. I nearly gave up completely a few times. Frankly... I lost my way. I didn't know how to communicate with my partner or my friends, and as a result I saw everything I held dear slip right through my fingers. My moral code was broken. I tried so hard to make things right... to make my relationships work. But I suppose I needed to learn some hard lessons first. Such is life.

My father passed away a few weeks ago. It was a slow progression: Cancer took him but, on its own timeline. I'm heartbroken. So much of how I want to be, I've modeled after him. He was stable. Calm. Measured. Loyal. Honorable. I miss him so much. But I am also thankful. Because he was sick, I got to spend 5 precious months with him. I cherished every moment. The kind ones. the infuriating ones. It wasn't always pretty, but i'm glad I had the time. I'm glad we had the time.

I'm tired of being sad. Tired of being broken. I want to live my life with joy and purpose. To spread that to others the way I once did in the past. I put this site together to remind myself that life is good. That there is joy in the world and that people deserve a chance to shine. No more judgement or self pity. My hope is this is the first and last of the downer posts. I don't owe it to the world... But I do owe it to Dad. To be the man that he was. To be better and to raise the bar. Wish me luck world... I step into tomorrow with bright eyes and an open heart.
