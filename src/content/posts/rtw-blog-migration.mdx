Once upon a time, my ex wife and I quit our jobs and traveled around the world. In hindsight, it was probably a reckless move that cost us a stomach churning amount of money. At least in opportunity cost. But I feel like it was a defining moment in my life. Something that allowed me to envision life paths that were previously unheard of. For that I am thankful.

I blogged about our experience on the road... but somewhere along the way the tools and the services that i originally used ceased to exist. This has always bugged me... but I never had a good excuse to take the time to fix things and port the posts to a more stable site. Until now. As I have been putting together this site, it occurred to me that I had a wealth of test data sitting right there. All I had to do was port everything... and AI would actually do most of the work for me!

So I hope that you enjoy the musings of a (then) Thirty-Something year old man going through an early mid-life crisis. It certainly has brought back fond memories for me... and I think, still has some valuable insights. Even today. Enjoy:
